Help & Support

How to Manage Ambulance

Managing your ambulance details is easy and essential for ensuring quick responses to emergencies:

  • Go to the Manage Ambulance section in your dashboard.
  • To add a new ambulance, click the Add Ambulance button, fill in the required details (ambulance number, driver name, contact info, etc.), and click Save.
  • To edit existing ambulance details, click on the Edit button next to the ambulance entry you wish to modify.
  • To remove an ambulance, click the Delete button, and confirm the deletion.

How to Manage Notifications

You can customize your notification settings to stay updated on important events:

  • Navigate to the Notification Settings section in your account settings.
  • Toggle on or off notifications for events like new ambulance requests, emergencies, and system updates.
  • You can also choose how you want to receive notifications (email, SMS, or in-app notifications).
  • After selecting your preferences, click Save Settings to apply your changes.

How to Manage Account Settings

Keep your account up-to-date to ensure smooth communication and secure access:

  • Go to the Account Settings section in your profile.
  • To change your personal information, click the Edit Profile button and update your name, email, phone number, etc.
  • To change your password, click on the Change Password button and follow the prompts to set a new password.
  • After making your changes, click Save to confirm your updates.
